Organizing your life doesn’t have to be hard

You’re not bad at organizing your tasks, you just didn’t use the right tools before.

  •  Your handwritten to do lists have a tendency to disappear and to be wherever you were not
  • You have to choose between using your desktop and your phone to organize your digital tasks.
  • You are worried about privacy when you're using apps developed by big companies

You can do it

Memini helps you to get things DONE.

  •  It has four lists to keep your tasks organised and tasks can be easily switched from one list to another.
  • It has 6 categories with different colors to assign to different types of tasks.
  • You can update the task description, task category, the task state (done or not done) and you can delete tasks.
  • It is fully responsive to the size of your device. You can also use your phone or tablet to manage your tasks.

Welcome to memini

The answer to my useless to do lists

I tried many 'to do list' apps and websites in the past years, but all had their limitations. Furthermore, I found that I did not feel comfortable by using apps from big companies such as Google.

When I rediscovered my passion for web development, this was automatically the first thing I wanted to build for myself.

Completing this first project involved learning various modern techniques and tools, including Figma, advanced CSS3, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB and much more. Currently, the Memini project is not completely finished, but I'm determined to finish it someday soon.
